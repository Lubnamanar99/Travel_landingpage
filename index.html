TOP DESTINATIONS

PARIS

The City of Light draws millions of visitors every year with its unforgettable ambiance.Containing world-class museums, fashion, cuisine and an atmosphere all its own, Paris is also a city of "many splendors,".

Read more
AMALFI COAST

One look at the Amalfi Coast and you may believe that you've found heaven on earth. That's the kind of spellbinding effect this stretch of Italian coastline tends to have on the 5 million annual visitors who cross its mesmerizing paths.

Read more
AMSTERDAM

At some point, during an excellent Indonesian meal, a twilight canal-side rambling or a shopping excursion through the boutiques of Nine Little Streets, you'll realize as many travelers have before you that there's much more to Amsterdam than you might've thought.

Read more
LONDON

Life in London is nothing short of invigorating, and travelers find that one visit isn't enough to experience everything this two-millennia-old city has to offer.Here, the antiquated clasps hands with the contemporary. You'll find the historic Tower of London and the avant-garde Tate Modern.

Read more
BERLIN

More than two decades since the fall of the Berlin Wall, the city basks in a cultural renaissance that boasts everything from museums and fashion to food and nightclubs.

Read more
CYPRUS

This island situated off the southern coast of Turkey isn't as popular with foreigners as other European beach and island destinations farther west, though it is favored by Europeans. The island nation offers a mix of historical sites and natural attractions that make it an excellent vacation spot.

Read more
VENICE

Travelers of all kinds will appreciate Venice, Italy's enchanting canals, historical architecture and delectable eateries. Visitors can view opulent architecture at St. Mark's Basilica, stroll across the famous Rialto Bridge or snap a photo in front of the iconic Bridge of Sighs.

Read more
ROME

Rome, the city of seven hills, enjoyed a mythic beginning.Italy's capital city, Rome is also known for a history that dates back to the eras of Octavian, Julius Caesar and Hadrian, among others. Left behind are structures like the Pantheon, the Roman Forum and dozens of churches, among other historic gems.

Read more
SWISS ALPS

Located south of Zurich, Bern and Basel, the Swiss Alps are a haven for winter sports enthusiasts. While skiing, hiking and snowboarding tend to dominate this region, you'll also discover quaint towns, serene lakes and gorgeous scenery.

Read more
PRAGUE

A vacation in Prague will leave you feeling as if you've stepped into a fairy tale. Take in the Gothic architecture and vibrant atmosphere of Old Town Square, and be sure to tour Prague Castle and go for a stroll across the famously scenic Charles Bridge. Prague's affordable food and drinks, accommodations and public transportation make it a great place for travelers who wish to visit Europe on a budget.

Read more
SANTORINI

Visit this Greek island for its unique volcanic landscape, relaxing beaches and distinct local architecture. Enjoy stunning views of the striking blue sea and whitewashed buildings Santorini is famous for from the sky-high village of Oia.

Read more
DUBLIN

The city is the largest in Ireland, and its fast-growing immigrant population brings people from all over the world. The influence of these cultures is evident in the diverse and vibrant culinary scene that can be found here. That said, Dublin has held on to some of its classic characteristics. Known for its traditions, fine literature, folklore, customary music and dance, and fresh pints of Guinness are never in short supply.
